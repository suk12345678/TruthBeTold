Privacy Policy for TruthBeTold

Last Updated: January 23, 2026

Introduction

TruthBeTold ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Rent Amount: Your actual monthly rent payment
  • Income: Your monthly income
  • ZIP Code: Your residential ZIP code
  • Bedroom Count: Number of bedrooms in your unit
  • Unit Quality: Your assessment of your unit's quality (1-5 scale)

Automatically Collected Information

  • Anonymous User ID: A randomly generated identifier stored on your device
  • Market Rent Data: Fair Market Rent data for your area
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics about app usage

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Calculate rent fairness scores
  • Provide market rent comparisons
  • Improve our market rent estimates through aggregated user data
  • Analyze app performance and user experience

Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored securely using Supabase (PostgreSQL database)
  • We use industry-standard encryption for data transmission
  • Your data is associated with an anonymous user ID, not your personal identity
  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or phone numbers

Data Validation and Aggregation

  • User-submitted rent data may be aggregated with other submissions
  • Aggregated data (10+ submissions from 10+ unique users) may be used to improve market rent estimates
  • Individual submissions are never shared or sold
  • All aggregation is anonymous and privacy-preserving

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Database and backend services
  • HUD (U.S. Department of Housing and Urban Development): Fair Market Rent data
  • RentCast API: Additional market rent data

These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of information.

Data Retention

  • User submissions are retained indefinitely for market analysis
  • You can request deletion of your data by contacting us
  • Anonymous user IDs cannot be linked back to individuals

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of data collection by uninstalling the app

Children's Privacy

TruthBeTold is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
